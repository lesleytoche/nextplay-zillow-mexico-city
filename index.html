About the Event

Where Culture, Career & Community Collide

Nextplay LIVE is an experiential networking mixer backed by a global network of 40,000+ tech professionals. We bring together top talent with career opportunities at leading companies. Now in its 3rd year in Mexico City, this edition presented by Zillow features an unforgettable evening of executive talks, live entertainment, complimentary food and drinks, and direct networking with Zillow's hiring teams.

Whether you're exploring new career paths or expanding your professional network, this is where authentic connections happen. This event is open to all.
